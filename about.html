Who We Are?

We are Group 1 ICT from 12-Newton, dedicated to developing innovative technological solutions for real-world problems. As a team of passionate students specializing in information and communications technology, we aim to enhance efficiency through digital transformation. Our capstone project, Campus’Stream, is a web-based system designed to optimize bookstore operations within Montessori De Sagrada Familia Inc. We believe in the power of technology to streamline processes and improve user experiences. Through this project, we hope to contribute to a more convenient and organized school environment.

What is Campus'Stream?

Campus’Stream is an integrated management system designed to improve the efficiency of the Montessori De Sagrada Familia Inc. bookstore. The platform provides real-time inventory tracking, ensuring students can easily check product availability. It also streamlines transaction processes, reducing waiting times and enhancing bookstore management. The system features order management, automated notifications, and a secure database, making operations smoother and more reliable. By digitizing bookstore functions, Campus’Stream aims to create a faster, more organized, and user-friendly experience for students and staff.
